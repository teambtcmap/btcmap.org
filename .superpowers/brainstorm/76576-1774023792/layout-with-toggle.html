Panel States

List View

Search + category filters + merchant list. Click a merchant → transitions to detail view.

Detail View

Replaces list in same panel. "← Back" returns to list. Same ◀ toggle to collapse.

Collapsed

Panel hidden. Small ▶ button at left edge. Click to restore previous view (list or detail).

Wide screen (≥ 1536px) exception

On ultrawide monitors, list + detail show side-by-side (current behavior but with compact design). The ◀ toggle still collapses both panels.
